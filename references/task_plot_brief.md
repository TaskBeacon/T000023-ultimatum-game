# Task Plot Brief

## Task

- Title: Ultimatum Game
- Construct: fairness decision making / social reward processing
- Paradigm: responder-side accept/reject decisions for monetary offers.

## Rows

- Fair offer: proposer 5, responder 5.
- Unfair offer: proposer 7, responder 3.
- Very unfair offer: proposer 9, responder 1.

## Trial Timeline

1. Offer cue: 500 ms neutral pre-offer cue, no response.
2. Pre-decision fixation: 600 ms fixation, no response.
3. Offer decision: 2000 ms. Show proposer/responder shares. Press F to accept or J to reject.
4. Decision confirmation: 600 ms. Show accept, reject, or timeout.
5. Payoff feedback: 1000 ms. Show trial payoff and cumulative earnings.
6. ITI: 800 ms fixation, no response.

## Notes

- Accepting earns the responder share.
- Rejecting or timing out earns 0.
